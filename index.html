CollabU

This mobile application and website are used to find team partners for participating in college competitions.

Within the app, students can search for partners by creating team vacancies according to their needs. Students recruiting members can view the skills and other information of applicants who want to join their team vacancies. Additionally, students can also get competition information through the app. This application serves two stakeholders: students for the mobile app and universities for the website. Universities use this application to add competition information. The app is developed using Flutter, Laravel, and PostgreSQL database.
